PAYROSA — STELLAR APAC HACKATHON SUBMISSION


PROJECT NAME

PayRosa


PROJECT DESCRIPTION

PayRosa is pay-by-link invoicing for freelancers, settled on Stellar. A
freelancer connects a wallet, creates an invoice, and shares a public payment
link. The client opens the link, connects a wallet, and pays on-chain. Funds
land directly in the freelancer's Stellar wallet in seconds with a real
transaction hash. XLM is the default settlement asset so any funded wallet can
pay with no trustline; USDC is available as a one-tap opt-in.


PROBLEM STATEMENT

Freelancers who work across borders wait days and lose 5 to 10 percent on every
payout through wires, PayPal, or Payoneer. Moving to crypto usually swaps one
problem for another: a missing trustline, the wrong network, an op_no_trust
error, and a client who gives up. The result is a payment that should take
seconds taking a week.


PROPOSED SOLUTION

1. The freelancer connects a Stellar wallet using a SEP-10 style challenge that
   is signed and verified server-side. The workspace is provisioned on connect.
2. The freelancer creates an invoice and picks an asset. XLM is pre-selected
   because native payments require no trustline.
3. PayRosa generates a public pay page and a SEP-7 QR code per invoice.
4. The client opens the link, connects a wallet, and pays. The server verifies
   the signed transaction matches the invoice destination, asset, and amount,
   submits it to Horizon, and marks the invoice paid with the confirmed hash.
5. The freelancer can pay earnings out to any Stellar address as a real
   on-chain transfer.


TARGET USERS / AUDIENCE

Independent freelancers, contractors, and small studios in the Philippines and
the wider APAC region who invoice cross-border clients and want fast, low-fee
settlement without bank delays or chargebacks.


SELECTED FOCUS TRACK

Payments and Commerce.


EXPECTED STELLAR INTEGRATION

1. Soroban escrow smart contract (payrosa-escrow, soroban-sdk 22) that custodies
   an invoice between deposit, release and refund via the native XLM Stellar
   Asset Contract. Deployed and initialized on testnet.
   Contract ID: CABRI2VIB5OMWHOTXPGSY473OMSCIYHW4OJB6N2G66IYYO5COUH3233X
2. SEP-10 style wallet authentication: a challenge transaction is signed by the
   wallet and the signature is verified on the server before a session is set.
3. Native XLM and issued-asset USDC via Stellar Asset Contracts; USDC is enabled
   with an in-app changeTrust.
4. SEP-7 payment URIs generated per invoice for QR-based wallet payment.
5. Soroban RPC: simulate, assemble, submit and poll the contract invocations.
   The client funds the escrow; funds can only release to the freelancer or
   refund the client, so a settlement cannot be faked or mis-routed.
