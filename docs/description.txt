PAYROSA — SHORT DESCRIPTION

PayRosa is pay-by-link invoicing for freelancers, settled through a Soroban
escrow smart contract on Stellar. Create an invoice, share the link, and your
client funds an on-chain escrow with one signature; the contract instantly
releases the payout to your wallet with a real transaction hash. XLM is the
default settlement asset so any funded wallet can pay with no trustline, and
USDC is a one-tap opt-in. No banks, no multi-day waits, no chargebacks.

TRACK

Payments and Commerce.

STELLAR FEATURES USED

1. Soroban escrow smart contract (deposit / release / refund) custodying the
   invoice amount via the native XLM Stellar Asset Contract.
   Contract: CABRI2VIB5OMWHOTXPGSY473OMSCIYHW4OJB6N2G66IYYO5COUH3233X (testnet).
2. SEP-10 style wallet authentication (sign and verify a challenge).
3. Native XLM and issued-asset USDC via Stellar Asset Contracts.
4. In-app USDC trustline (changeTrust) opt-in.
5. SEP-7 payment URIs and QR codes per invoice.
6. Soroban RPC: simulate, assemble, submit and poll contract invocations.
