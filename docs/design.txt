PAYROSA — DESIGN NOTES


ELEVATOR PITCH

Invoice clients and get paid in seconds, on-chain. PayRosa turns an invoice into
a Stellar payment link. Your client opens it, connects a wallet, and the money
lands directly in your wallet with a verifiable transaction hash.


PROBLEM

Cross-border freelancer payouts are slow and expensive. Wires and remittance
apps take days and skim 5 to 10 percent. Asking clients to use crypto usually
fails on a missing trustline or the wrong network, so the payment stalls.


SOLUTION

A pay-by-link invoice settled on Stellar. The freelancer connects once, creates
an invoice, and shares a link. The client pays on-chain from any Stellar wallet.
XLM is the default asset because native payments need no trustline, so a payer
is never blocked. USDC is one tap away through an in-app changeTrust. The server
verifies each signed payment matches the invoice before broadcasting it, so a
settlement is always real and always provable.


DEMO FLOW

1. Open the landing page and choose Connect wallet.
2. Sign the SEP-10 challenge. The dashboard opens with the wallet chip showing.
3. Create an invoice: client name, description, amount, asset (XLM default).
4. Open the public pay link and pay with a connected wallet.
5. The invoice flips to paid and shows a stellar.expert transaction link.
6. Visit Network stats to see the real interaction counts update.


WOW MOMENT

The pay page settles the invoice with a single tap and immediately shows a real,
clickable transaction hash on stellar.expert. There is no simulated success: the
amount moved on the Stellar network and you can verify it yourself.


VISUAL IDENTITY

Teal-forward palette with a deep teal primary and warm off-white surfaces.
Space Grotesk for headings, DM Sans for body. Rounded cards, generous spacing,
one clear primary action per screen, and designed loading, empty, and error
states throughout.
